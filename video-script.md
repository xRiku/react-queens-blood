# Queen's Blood — Update Showcase Video Script

**Format:** 45-60 second highlight reel
**Music:** Upbeat lo-fi or synth-wave track (royalty-free). Suggestions:
- [Uppbeat](https://uppbeat.io/) — search "gaming lo-fi" or "retro synth"
- [Pixabay Music](https://pixabay.com/music/) — search "game trailer" or "electronic chill"
- The FF7 Rebirth Queen's Blood theme if fair use allows (risky for monetized content)

**Sound Design:** Layer these on top of the music:
- Satisfying UI click/whoosh on each cut transition
- The in-game sounds (card flick, placement, game-start chord) during gameplay clips
- A subtle bass drop or riser before the final CTA

---

## Shot List

### 1. Hook — 0:00-0:03
**Visual:** Quick montage — 3 fast cuts (0.5s each): card being placed → buff effect rippling → fireworks on win
**Audio:** Music starts with energy. Quick whoosh on each cut.
**Text overlay:** "Queen's Blood got a major update."

### 2. Play Instantly — 0:03-0:08
**Visual:** Screen recording: open the website → type a name → click "Play vs Bot" → game loads instantly
**Audio:** Music continues. In-game "game start" chord plays.
**Text overlay:** "No download. No setup. Just play."

### 3. Card Effects — 0:08-0:18
**Visual:** Place a Crystalline Crab — show the buff indicator light up on adjacent tiles. Then place a Cactuar — show it destroying an enemy card. Show the tile-level buff/debuff indicators glowing.
**Audio:** Card flick sound on each placement. A satisfying impact sound on card destruction.
**Text overlay:** "Cards now have real effects — buffs, debuffs, destruction."

### 4. Placement Preview — 0:18-0:23
**Visual:** Hover over different tiles with a card selected — show the green valid-tile rings, then hover to preview exactly what will happen before placing.
**Audio:** Hover sounds from the game. Quick whoosh.
**Text overlay:** "Preview every move before you commit."

### 5. Multiplayer — 0:23-0:32
**Visual:** Create a room → show the 6-digit code → second device joins with the code → ready room with both players confirming → match starts
**Audio:** Music builds. UI click sounds.
**Text overlay:** "6-digit codes. Ready room. Rematch in one click."

### 6. Sound & Polish — 0:32-0:38
**Visual:** Quick montage of: sound toggle, card hover sounds, the synth game-start chord, fireworks with explosion SFX on a win
**Audio:** Let the in-game sounds be the star — mute the background music briefly for the game-start chord, then bring it back.
**Text overlay:** "Full sound design. Every action feels right."

### 7. Deck Builder — 0:38-0:44
**Visual:** Open deck builder → scroll through cards catalog → drag/tap cards into deck slots → show the 5×3 grid filling up
**Audio:** Card flick on each card added.
**Text overlay:** "Browse every card. Build your deck."

### 8. CTA — 0:44-0:50
**Visual:** Fade to the home screen of the game. The patch notes page scrolls up briefly in a picture-in-picture corner.
**Audio:** Music resolves. Subtle riser into final beat.
**Text overlay (large, centered):**
  "Play now — [your-url]"
  (smaller) "Full patch notes on the site"

## Editing Notes

- **Pacing:** Keep cuts tight — no shot longer than 5 seconds. The hook should feel punchy.
- **Transitions:** Simple cuts or quick zooms. No star wipes.
- **Music sync:** Time cuts to the beat if possible — this is the single biggest thing that makes a short video feel professional.
- **Resolution:** Record at 1920×1080. Export 1080p for YouTube, 1080×1920 vertical crop for TikTok/Reels/Shorts.
- **Sound mixing:** Music at ~60% volume, in-game SFX at 100%, transition whooshes at ~40%.
- **Captions:** Add text overlays as listed above — many people watch without sound initially.

## Where to Find Free SFX

- **Transition whooshes:** [Pixabay SFX](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/) — search "whoosh" or "swoosh"
- **UI clicks:** [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org/) — search "UI click"
- **Bass risers:** Pixabay SFX — search "cinematic riser"
